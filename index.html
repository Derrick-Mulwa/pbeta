Hello, I'm Derrick Mulwa, a passionate and results-driven Data Scientist, Data Analyst, and Python Developer with three years of extensive hands-on experience in delivering innovative solutions across various industries. With a strong foundation in data analysis, financial analysis, data visualization, machine learning, and automation, I specialize in transforming complex data into actionable insights and efficient workflows.

I have deep expertise in Python programming, leveraging libraries such as pandas, numpy, tensorflow, sklearn, matplotlib, SQLAlchemy, selenium, statsmodel among others to build robust data models and analytical tools. Additionally, I am proficient in using Power BI to design intuitive and interactive dashboards that drive businesses decisions, Microsoft Excel, and SQL (MySQL, SQLServer) to design, create, extract data, and optimize databases.

My technical skills also extend into automation, where I have experience in Power Automate, Python automation, and creating custom scripts for process efficiency.

I am currently focused on offering tailored services to companies and individuals, including data analysis, machine learning solutions, and process automation tasks.

Whether you're looking to gain insights from data, optimize business processes, or automate routine tasks, I'm here to help bring your vision to life.

Feel free to get in touch, and let's work together to achieve your business goals!
